Our Story

Founded with a love for artisanal coffee and handcrafted pastries, Lola’s Latte & Layers was born from the idea of creating a warm, welcoming space for coffee lovers and dessert enthusiasts alike. We believe that great coffee and delicious treats have the power to bring people together and create memorable moments.
